Bold — Priority Banner

The top of Today should be the page's hero: one slot that always holds the single most important thing, and is the only filled-color block above the fold. That contrast — one tinted hero over all-white cards — is what makes it stand out, without alarming anyone.

1 · The system — one slot, a priority order

The banner shows exactly one thing. What fills it is decided by this hierarchy (top wins). When the top item is resolved — e.g. the daily med check is done — the banner falls through to the next. Color encodes urgency; only red is alarming, everything else is calm-but-elevated.

1
Safety alert
Severe symptom logged, hold-dose instruction, overdue critical lab.
Urgent · red
2
Daily medication check-in
On medication & not checked in today. A safety touchpoint — prominent but reassuring.
Calm · teal
3
Time-sensitive action
Appointment today (join), lab due, refill review approaching.
Attention · amber
4
Onboarding next step
Pre-visit states — the next setup task to keep momentum.
Guide · purple
5
Encouragement
Nothing urgent — a warm highlight (a streak, a win) so the hero is never empty.
Positive · green
