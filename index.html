03 — The Problem We Solve

Uncertainty blocks action

Two connected problems — one for users, one for the business — that a unified adaptive system can solve.

User Problem

Uncertainty blocks action

  • "Is this exercise safe for me?"
  • "Am I doing enough?"
  • "Should I change something?"
  • "Is this pain normal?"
  • "What did my doctor mean?"
Business Problem

Fragmentation limits growth

  • Two separate products (Fitness + Care) create friction, confusion, and support burden
  • Static program model doesn't adapt to daily reality (pain flares, fatigue, stress)
  • Need clear clinical measurement framework for Medicare ACCESS (MSK) reimbursement
  • Current design creates evaluation anxiety rather than reducing uncertainty

07 — Research-Backed Design

Grounded in behavioral science

Our design principles are informed by evidence on exercise adherence and behavior change in older adults.

📊

Adherence Factors

  • Supervised programs show higher adherence (65–86%) vs unsupervised (58–77%)
  • Person-level factors: better health, fewer medications
  • Physical factors: better functional ability
  • Psychological: better cognitive ability, fewer depressive symptoms
🔬

Behavior Change Techniques

  • Goal-setting & self-monitoring most effective
  • Feedback & social support enhance adherence
  • Problem-solving reduces barriers
  • Personalized feedback > generic messaging

Design implication: Bold acts as a "supervised" companion — offering the structure, feedback, and reassurance that drive adherence, while adapting to each person's daily reality.

08 — Information Architecture

Structuring Bold's experience

Five primary surfaces. Simple, predictable navigation. Progress signals woven throughout.

📋

Today

One clear action. Completion clarity. Lightweight reassurance.

🔍

Discover

On-demand, live, articles, audio, meditation — matched to your program.

❤️

Care

Telehealth. Nutrition, exercise, sleep, stress, and provider support.

👤

Profile

Progress narrative, check-in history, program settings.

📋

Today's Plan

  • Answers "What do I do right now?"
  • One clear action at a time
  • Provides completion clarity
  • Lightweight reassurance and adjustment visibility
📝

Check-ins & Progress

  • Baseline, periodic, and triggered check-ins
  • Embedded signals in Today and Profile summaries
  • Hybrid model balances clarity and lightness
  • Progress shown as narrative, not percentages

Architecture principles: Simplify navigation. Align with care expansion. Support flexible future adjustments. Progress signals live in Today, Check-ins, and Profile — never behind extra taps.
